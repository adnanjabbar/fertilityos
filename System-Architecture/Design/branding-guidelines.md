# Branding Guidelines — FertilityOS

## Brand Identity

**FertilityOS** is the world's first comprehensive operating system for fertility clinics and reproductive health centers. The brand communicates:

- **Clinical precision** — trustworthy, accurate, professional
- **Human warmth** — empathetic, life-affirming, hopeful
- **Technological innovation** — modern, cloud-native, intelligent

---

## Brand Name

- **Full name:** FertilityOS
- **Short form:** FOS (internal only)
- **Domain:** fertilityo.com (primary), fertilityo.s (alternate)
- **Tagline:** *The Operating System for Fertility Care*
- **Alternate tagline:** *Empowering Fertility Clinics. Transforming Outcomes.*

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo
- Wordmark: "FertilityOS" in Geist / Inter typeface
- Symbol: A stylized cell/bloom icon representing a fertilized egg or new life — combining medical precision with biological beauty
- The "OS" suffix uses the brand accent color (Teal Green) to reinforce the software-platform identity

### Logo Usage Rules
- Minimum size: 120px wide (digital), 30mm (print)
- Clear space: Equal to the height of the "O" in "OS" on all sides
- **Do not** stretch, rotate, recolor, or apply drop shadows to the logo
- On dark backgrounds, use the white version
- On light backgrounds, use the primary version

## Voice & Tone

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Marketing / Landing page | Confident, warm, inspiring |
| Onboarding | Friendly, clear, encouraging |
| Clinical module UI | Professional, precise, neutral |
| Error messages | Direct, non-blaming, helpful |
| Billing / Finance | Clear, trustworthy, formal |

### Writing Principles
1. **Lead with benefit, not feature** — "Track every embryo" not "Embryo tracking module"
2. **Use active voice** — "You manage patients" not "Patients are managed"
3. **Be specific** — "Handle 500+ patients per clinic" not "Handle many patients"
4. **Empathize with the mission** — Fertility care is deeply personal. Acknowledge it.
5. **Avoid jargon** — Technical enough for clinicians, accessible enough for administrators

---

## Photography & Illustration Style

- **Photography:** Real clinical environments, diverse staff and patients, high-resolution, warm natural lighting
- **Illustration:** Flat, soft-gradient illustrations of medical concepts (cells, DNA, fertility cycle)
- **No stock photo clichés** — Avoid overly generic doctor-pointing-at-screen images
- **Medical imagery:** Should feel precise and scientific, not clinical-cold
