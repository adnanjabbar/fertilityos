# Problem Discovery — FertilityOS

## The Problem

Fertility clinics and reproductive health centers are among the most complex medical environments to manage. Unlike general practice, fertility care involves:

- **Multi-week stimulation protocols** that require daily monitoring and protocol adjustments
- **IVF laboratory workflows** — egg retrieval, fertilization, embryo culture, grading, cryopreservation
- **High-stakes data integrity** — an embryo misidentification is catastrophic
- **Deeply personal patient journeys** — emotional, financial, and physical burden on patients
- **Multi-disciplinary coordination** — fertility specialists, embryologists, nurses, lab technicians, radiologists all working on one patient simultaneously
- **Regulatory complexity** — clinics must comply with national fertility authority regulations and maintain detailed audit trails

### The Gap

Despite this complexity, most fertility clinics today rely on:
- General-purpose EMR software (built for GPs, not IVF labs)
- Spreadsheets for embryology tracking
- Paper-based cycle charts
- Disconnected billing and scheduling tools
- No unified platform for the full patient journey

**The result:** Data silos, manual errors, inefficiency, staff burnout, poor patient experience, and compliance risk.
