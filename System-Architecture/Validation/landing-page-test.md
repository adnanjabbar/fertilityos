# Landing Page Validation — FertilityOS

## Objective

Use the landing page at `website/` to validate demand for FertilityOS before investing in full product development. This is the "Landing Page Test" phase of the Validation framework.

---

## Hypothesis

> "Fertility clinic administrators and owners will sign up for early access to a modern, cloud-native platform purpose-built for IVF workflows."

---

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Unique visitors | 1,000 | Month 1 |
| Waitlist signups | 100 | Month 1 |
| Conversion rate (visitor → signup) | ≥ 10% | Month 1 |
| Email open rate (follow-up) | ≥ 40% | Month 1 |
| Demo requests | 20 | Month 1 |

---

## Traffic Sources (Planned)

1. **LinkedIn** — target fertility specialists and clinic administrators
2. **Fertility conference communities** — ESHRE, ASRM member groups
3. **Cold email** — target fertility clinic office managers
4. **SEO** — "fertility clinic software", "IVF management system"
5. **Product Hunt** — developer and SaaS audience validation

---

## Landing Page A/B Tests (Planned)

| Test | Variant A | Variant B |
|---|---|---|
| Headline | "The Operating System for Fertility Care" | "Manage Your Entire Fertility Clinic in One Place" |
| CTA | "Get Early Access" | "Start Free Trial" |
| Social proof | Clinic count | Waitlist count |
| Hero | Dashboard mockup | Feature walkthrough video |

---

## Waitlist Follow-up Sequence

1. **Day 0** — Confirmation email: "You're on the list! Here's what's coming."
2. **Day 3** — Product teaser: "A sneak peek at the IVF Lab module"
3. **Day 7** — Survey: "Tell us about your clinic's biggest challenge"
4. **Day 14** — Offer: "Book a 20-minute discovery call"
5. **Day 21** — Social proof: "X clinics have joined the waitlist"

---

## Feedback Collection

Waitlist form captures:
- Clinic name (optional)
- Work email (required)
- Role (Clinic Owner/Admin, Doctor, Manager, Other) — to be added in v2
- Biggest challenge (free text) — to be added in v2

---

## Pre-Sales Strategy

Once 100 waitlist signups are achieved:
1. Offer **Founding Clinic** deals at 50% off first year
2. Close 3 paying customers before writing a line of product code
3. Use revenue to fund Phase 2 (Account Creation) development
