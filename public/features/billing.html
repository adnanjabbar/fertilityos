Billing & Finance

Integrated invoicing, payment processing, and financial reporting designed specifically for fertility clinics and IVF centers.

Complete Financial Management for Fertility Clinics

FertilityOS combines invoicing, payment collection, and financial reporting in a single platform. Whether you accept credit cards through Stripe, manual payments, or insurance, everything is tracked and reconciled automatically.

Invoicing & Billing

Create and send professional invoices for IVF cycles, procedures, lab services, and consultations. Automatically generate itemized bills based on treatment plans and track payment status in real time.

  • Automated invoice generation from treatment records
  • Itemized billing for procedures, labs, and medications
  • Customizable invoice templates
  • Patient billing portal for payment history
  • Recurring billing for storage and monitoring fees

Payment Processing

Accept payments through multiple channels including Stripe for credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash/cheque. All payment methods are unified in a single dashboard for easy reconciliation.

  • Stripe integration for card payments
  • Support for cash, cheque, and bank transfers
  • Automatic payment confirmation and receipts
  • Payment plan and installment support
  • Multi-currency support for international clinics

Insurance & Coverage Tracking

Track insurance authorizations, coverage limits, and claim submissions. Manage prior authorizations for IVF procedures and monitor benefit utilization.

  • Insurance provider and plan management
  • Prior authorization tracking
  • Coverage limit monitoring
  • Claim submission documentation

Financial Reporting

Generate comprehensive financial reports including revenue summaries, accounts receivable aging, payment collection rates, and profitability analysis by procedure type.
