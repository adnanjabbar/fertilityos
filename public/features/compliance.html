Compliance & Audit Logs

HIPAA-ready security, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit trails to keep your fertility clinic compliant and your patient data protected.

Healthcare Compliance Built Into Every Layer

FertilityOS is built from the ground up with healthcare compliance in mind. Every feature, from user authentication to data storage, incorporates security best practices and regulatory requirements for fertility clinics operating worldwide.

Role-Based Access Control

FertilityOS provides 22 configurable roles with granular, module-level permissions. Ensure that each team member — from clinic directors to front-desk staff — has access only to the data and functions they need.

  • 22 pre-configured roles (Clinic Director, Embryologist, Nurse, etc.)
  • Module-level permission granularity
  • Custom role creation and modification
  • User invitation with role assignment
  • Automatic session management and timeout

Comprehensive Audit Logging

Every action in FertilityOS is logged with a timestamp, user identity, and detailed record of what changed. Audit logs are immutable and searchable, providing a complete trail for regulatory audits and internal reviews.

  • Immutable, timestamped audit trail
  • User identity and action tracking
  • Before/after change documentation
  • Searchable and filterable log interface
  • Export-ready audit reports

Data Security & Encryption

Patient data is protected with industry-standard encryption both in transit and at rest. Secure authentication with JWT tokens, bcrypt password hashing, and configurable session policies ensures only authorized access.

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted data storage at rest
  • JWT-based secure authentication
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Rate limiting and brute-force protection

Regulatory Compliance Support

FertilityOS helps fertility clinics meet regulatory requirements across multiple jurisdictions. Built-in compliance features support HIPAA, GDPR, and local healthcare regulations applicable to reproductive medicine.

  • HIPAA-ready infrastructure and controls
  • GDPR data protection support
  • Consent management and documentation
  • Data retention policy configuration
  • Right-to-deletion request handling
