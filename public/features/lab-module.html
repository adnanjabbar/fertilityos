Advanced Embryology Lab Module

Complete laboratory information management for embryology teams — from oocyte retrieval through embryo grading, cryopreservation, and quality control.

Laboratory Management Built for Embryologists

The FertilityOS Lab Module is designed in collaboration with embryologists to provide a purpose-built laboratory information management system (LIMS) for IVF laboratories. Every workflow, from semen analysis to embryo vitrification, is streamlined for accuracy and efficiency.

Embryo Grading & Tracking

Record and track embryo development from fertilization through blastocyst stage. Use standardized grading systems with the flexibility to customize grading criteria to match your laboratory protocols.

  • Day-by-day embryo development tracking
  • Standardized and customizable grading systems
  • Embryo selection tools for transfer decisions
  • Visual timeline of embryo development
  • Gardner, Istanbul, or custom grading scales

Cryopreservation Management

Manage your cryo storage with detailed tracking of vitrification and thawing events, tank locations, canister positions, and sample identification. Built-in chain-of-custody ensures specimen safety.

  • Tank, canister, and position tracking
  • Vitrification and thawing event logging
  • Chain-of-custody documentation
  • Storage fee tracking and renewal reminders
  • Specimen identity verification workflows

Semen Analysis & Processing

Comprehensive semen analysis module supporting WHO criteria with automated calculations for motility, morphology, and concentration. Track all processing steps from collection to preparation.

  • WHO-standard semen analysis parameters
  • Automated motility and concentration calculations
  • Sperm preparation and wash documentation
  • Donor sperm tracking and quarantine management

Quality Control Workflows

Built-in QC workflows ensure laboratory standards are maintained. Track equipment calibration, media lot numbers, incubator conditions, and environmental monitoring with automated alerts for out-of-range values.

  • Equipment calibration tracking
  • Media and reagent lot management
  • Incubator and environment monitoring
  • Witness verification for critical steps
  • Competency assessment documentation
