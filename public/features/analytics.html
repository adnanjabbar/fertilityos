Data-Driven Fertility Clinic Management

FertilityOS analytics gives clinic directors and medical staff instant visibility into clinical outcomes, operational efficiency, and financial performance. Make informed decisions backed by real-time data from your clinic's operations.

Clinical Outcome Tracking

Track IVF success rates by protocol, physician, age group, and diagnosis. Monitor implantation rates, clinical pregnancy rates, and live birth rates over time to identify trends and optimize treatment approaches.

  • IVF success rate tracking by multiple dimensions
  • Implantation and pregnancy rate dashboards
  • Age-stratified outcome analysis
  • Protocol comparison and effectiveness reports
  • Benchmark comparison with industry standards

Operational Metrics

Monitor patient volumes, cycle counts, lab utilization, appointment scheduling efficiency, and staff productivity. Identify bottlenecks and optimize resource allocation.

  • Patient volume and growth trends
  • Cycle count and type distribution
  • Appointment utilization rates
  • Staff workload and productivity metrics
  • Lab equipment utilization tracking

Financial Reporting

Track revenue, payment collection rates, outstanding balances, and procedure profitability. Generate financial reports for accounting, management, and regulatory purposes.

  • Revenue tracking and forecasting
  • Payment collection rate analysis
  • Outstanding balance reports
  • Procedure-level profitability analysis
  • Export-ready financial reports

Custom Reports & Data Export

Build custom reports tailored to your clinic's needs. Export data in standard formats for regulatory submissions, research purposes, or integration with external systems.
