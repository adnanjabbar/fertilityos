FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FertilityOS. Can't find an answer? Contact our support team.

What is FertilityOS?

FertilityOS is a cloud-based fertility clinic management platform designed specifically for IVF centers, embryology labs, and reproductive medicine institutions. It provides comprehensive tools for patient management, cycle tracking, lab workflows, billing, analytics, and regulatory compliance.

Is FertilityOS HIPAA compliant?

Yes. FertilityOS is built with HIPAA-ready security including encrypted data storage, role-based access controls with 22 configurable roles, audit logging, and secure authentication. Our platform helps fertility clinics meet healthcare data protection requirements.

How long does it take to set up FertilityOS?

Most clinics are up and running within minutes. Simply sign up, configure your clinic settings, invite your team members, and start managing patients. Our onboarding wizard guides you through each step of the process.

Can FertilityOS handle multiple clinic locations?

Yes. FertilityOS supports multi-tenant architecture allowing you to manage multiple clinic locations from a single account with centralized reporting and location-specific configurations for each site.

What IVF protocols does FertilityOS support?

FertilityOS supports all major IVF protocols including conventional IVF, ICSI, IUI, frozen embryo transfer (FET), egg donation cycles, and surrogacy programs. The system is flexible enough to accommodate custom protocols specific to your practice.

Does FertilityOS include embryology lab management?

Yes. The advanced lab module includes embryo grading and tracking, cryopreservation management, semen analysis, oocyte tracking, quality control workflows, and chain-of-custody documentation for full lab oversight.
