Automated Notifications

Keep patients and staff informed with automated email notifications for appointments, cycle milestones, verifications, and important updates.

Automated Communication for Better Patient Engagement

FertilityOS automates routine clinic communications so your team can focus on patient care. From appointment reminders to cycle milestone updates, the right message reaches the right person at the right time — automatically.

Appointment Reminders

Reduce no-shows with automated appointment reminder emails sent at configurable intervals before scheduled visits. Patients receive clear details about their upcoming appointments with easy options to confirm or reschedule.

  • Configurable reminder timing (24h, 48h, 1 week)
  • Appointment details with location and preparation instructions
  • Confirmation and rescheduling links
  • Staff notification for appointment changes

Cycle Milestone Updates

Automatically notify patients and their partners about important cycle milestones — trigger day, retrieval results, fertilization reports, embryo development updates, and pregnancy test scheduling.

  • Trigger day notifications
  • Retrieval and fertilization result alerts
  • Embryo development status updates
  • Transfer scheduling confirmations
  • Pregnancy test result follow-ups

Email Verification & Security

Secure account creation with email verification tokens. Patients and staff receive verification emails during registration with time-limited tokens for account activation.

  • Account verification emails with secure tokens
  • 24-hour token expiration for security
  • Password reset notifications
  • Login activity alerts for suspicious access

Staff & Internal Notifications

Keep your clinical team informed with internal notifications for lab results, approval requests, schedule changes, and important patient events that require attention.

  • Lab result notifications for clinicians
  • Approval request alerts for supervisors
  • Schedule change notifications
  • Critical patient event alerts
  • Customizable notification preferences per user
