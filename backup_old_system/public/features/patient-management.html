Patient & Cycle Management

Track every patient journey from initial consultation through stimulation, retrieval, transfer, and pregnancy with detailed records and intelligent automation.

Complete Patient Lifecycle Tracking

FertilityOS provides a unified patient management system built specifically for fertility clinics. From the first consultation to pregnancy confirmation, every step of the patient's journey is documented, tracked, and accessible to authorized team members.

Patient Records & Intake

Create comprehensive patient profiles with guided intake forms that capture medical history, fertility history, partner information, and insurance details. Digital intake forms reduce paperwork and improve data accuracy from day one.

  • Guided patient intake forms with validation
  • Complete medical and fertility history tracking
  • Partner and donor profile management
  • Insurance and coverage information
  • Document upload and storage

IVF Cycle Management

Track every aspect of IVF, ICSI, IUI, and FET cycles with protocol-specific workflows. Monitor stimulation days, medication dosages, follicle measurements, hormone levels, and procedure outcomes in real time.

  • Support for IVF, ICSI, IUI, FET, and donor cycles
  • Day-by-day stimulation tracking with medication logs
  • Follicle and hormone monitoring dashboards
  • Retrieval, fertilization, and transfer documentation
  • Pregnancy test results and outcome tracking

Automated Alerts & Reminders

Never miss a critical milestone. FertilityOS automatically sends alerts for upcoming procedures, medication changes, follow-up appointments, and cycle milestones to both staff and patients.

  • Configurable appointment reminders
  • Cycle milestone notifications
  • Medication schedule alerts
  • Follow-up and outcome tracking reminders

Multi-Clinic Patient Access

For organizations with multiple locations, patient records are accessible across all clinics with proper authorization. Transfer patients between locations seamlessly while maintaining complete history.
